Hello, my name is Huzayll Muqrith Bin Israrr Ahmad and I'm 21 years old. I was born and raised in Klang, Selangor. I have always had a passion for art and expression. I skateboard, write rap lyrics, cut hair, and do graffiti art. I have been self-taught in developing these skills as I find it more enjoyable and fulfilling to learn things on my own. Skateboarding has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I love the freedom it gives me and the feeling of being in the moment. I take pride in the fact that I know how to do a few tricks and I am always looking for ways to improve. Writing rap lyrics is another passion of mine. I enjoy expressing myself through words and I find the challenge of coming up with clever rhymes and metaphors to be rewarding. I have been writing rap lyrics for a few years now and I'm proud of the progress I have made. Back in 2018, I performed at the 16 Baris Audition show and it was a cool experience. Graffiti art is something I find interesting to be able to create unique works of art that can be seen by many people or on social media. Although, I am constantly exploring new techniques and styles. I practice in my very own sketchbooks for new ideas. I'm happy with my creative pursuits and the progress I have made in each of these fields. I am grateful for the opportunity to express myself and to be able to share my art with others.
